Overview
The Strobe Demo is a program that simulates a rotating disc with an arrow, being flashed by a strobe light. The frequency of the motor is constant, and upon varying the flash rate, concepts such as aliasing can be observed.

Features:

  • Users can change the motor frequency and the sampling (flashing) rate.
  • Change the motor frequency and the sampling rate by using the keyboard.
  • Advance the slider with smaller steps by selecting radio boxes.
